About Luso Technical College

Luso Technical College is a Malawian technical and vocational training institution with campuses in Lilongwe and Blantyre. We train students in practical, job-ready skills that prepare them for employment, self-reliance, and entrepreneurship.

Our mission is to bridge the skills gap in Malawi by providing industry-relevant training that empowers youth to become productive members of society.
